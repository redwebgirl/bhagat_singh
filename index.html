The British government held the Simon Commission in 1928 to discuss autonomy for the Indias. But It was boycotted by several politicalmorganizations because this commission did not include any Indian representetive.

Because of Lathi charge police brutally hit the protestors. Lala Lajpat Rai got seriously injured and he was hospitalized. After few weeks Lala Ji became shaheed.
